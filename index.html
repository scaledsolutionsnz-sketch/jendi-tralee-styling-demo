About Jendi Tralee Styling

Personal styling that's personal

Jendi Tralee Styling is a one-woman studio in Christchurch, built on a simple belief: getting dressed should feel easy, and you should feel good in what you wear. Most of us don't need more clothes — we need the right ones, and a little guidance to put them together.

Every session is private, relaxed and tailored to you — your body, your lifestyle, your budget and your comfort. No judgement about what's in your wardrobe, and no pushing you toward a look that isn't you.

  • ✓ Clear, upfront session pricing — no surprises
  • ✓ Advice tailored to your shape, colouring and budget
