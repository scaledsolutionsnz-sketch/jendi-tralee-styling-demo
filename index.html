About

Personal styling that feels like getting dressed with a friend who knows fashion

Jendi Tralee Styling helps Christchurch women feel good in what they wear — without the overwhelm of trends, fast fashion or a wardrobe full of things that don't quite work.

It starts with you: your body, your routine, the way you want to be seen. From there it's about real, wearable choices you'll reach for again and again — and learning a few things along the way so you can keep doing it yourself.

  • Locally based, working one-on-one across Christchurch
  • Honest, judgement-free advice for every shape and budget
  • Styling that works with the wardrobe you already own
