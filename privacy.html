Privacy

How we handle the details you share with us.

Jendi Tralee Styling respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it, in line with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

What we collect

When you fill in an enquiry or feedback form, we collect the details you choose to share — typically your name, email, phone number and what you'd like help with. We only ask for what we need to respond to you.

How we use it

Your details are used solely to reply to your enquiry, arrange a session and provide our styling service. We don't sell your information, and we don't share it with third parties for marketing.

Storage

Enquiries are handled through our website's form provider and email. We keep your information only as long as needed to help you, and take reasonable care to keep it secure.

Your choices

You can ask to see, correct or delete the information we hold about you at any time. Just get in touch and we'll sort it.
